How Briefs Are Generated

Each Friday, generate_brief.py reads local data — VIX history, price history, backtest regime stats — and fills brief_template.md with this week's market snapshot, VIX gate status, candidate trades, open position flags, and next actions.

The sanitized version (shown here) strips dollar amounts and contract sizes. Run ./publish_brief.sh locally to push new entries.

Data: yfinance · Black-Scholes synthetic pricing · 2015–2025 backtest · Not financial advice.
